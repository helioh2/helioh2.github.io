Evaluation

If you would like to participate in our evaluation, please contact us! We can provide you with additional support, and may even be able to provide a stipend for qualifying schools.

The evaluation consists of the following:

  • Procedures and teacher/parent consent forms, for administering the pre- and post-tests.
  • Students should take the Algebra Pre- and Post-tests, as well as the Math Affect pre- and post surveys.
